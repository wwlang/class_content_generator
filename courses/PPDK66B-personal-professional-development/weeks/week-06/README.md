## Key Concepts

### Experiential Learning Focus

This week applies **Kolb's Experiential Learning Cycle** (from Week 3) in real-time:

**Stage 1: Concrete Experience** → The MISA visit itself
**Stage 2: Reflective Observation** → Field Trip Reflection Template (on bus, within 48 hours)
**Stage 3: Abstract Conceptualization** → Connecting to course frameworks (tutorials, essay writing)
**Stage 4: Active Experimentation** → Applying insights to PDP and career planning

### Observation Framework

Students observe MISA through **5 categories**:

1. **Physical Environment** - Office layout, technology, facilities, visual culture
2. **Organizational Culture** - Dress code, communication style, collaboration, work-life signals
3. **Professional Behaviors** - Employee interactions, meeting culture, problem-solving
4. **Career Pathways** - Roles, required skills, growth opportunities, entry points
5. **Values in Action** - Innovation, learning culture, employee engagement

### Connection to Self-Discovery (Week 5)

Students connect observations to their self-assessment results:
- MBTI/16 Personalities - personality fit with workplace culture
- VIA Character Strengths - which strengths would be valued
- Holland Code (RIASEC) - career interest alignment
- CliftonStrengths - how talents could contribute
- Learning Style - how they best absorbed the experience

## Timeline and Workflow

### Before Visit (Week 5-6)
- Students receive and read Field Trip Preparation Guide
- Review Kolb's Experiential Learning Cycle from Week 3
- Review Week 5 self-assessment results
- Prepare 3-5 thoughtful questions
- Print/access Field Trip Reflection Template

### During Visit (September 18)
- **Stage 1: Concrete Experience** - Engage fully in presentations, tour, Q&A
- Take detailed notes using 5-category observation framework
- Note emotional responses and surprises
- Ask prepared questions

### Immediately After (Bus Ride)
- **Stage 2: Reflective Observation** - Begin Field Trip Reflection Template
- Complete Parts 1-5 while memories are fresh (15-30 minutes)
- Capture specific quotes, moments, observations

### Within 48 Hours
- Complete entire Field Trip Reflection Template
- Send thank-you email to host (optional but encouraged)
- Schedule next mentorship meeting to discuss MISA
- Begin connecting observations to PDP draft

### Week 7 Tutorial
- Brief debrief: share one observation connecting to transferable skills/EI
- Bridge to Week 7 content on skills development

### Week 10+ (Post Guest Speaker)
- Begin drafting Reflective Essay using template as source material
- Compare/connect MISA insights with guest speaker insights

### Week 11 (PDP Due)
- Integrate MISA observations into SWOT, Skills Gap, Career Goals
- Reference in Mentor Integration section
- Use as evidence of industry research

### Exam Period (Final Oral Exam)
- Reference MISA in presentation to demonstrate real-world grounding
