**Slide 17: Strategy 1 - Avoid Predictability**
Layout: Content with visual
Title: Strategy 1: Avoid Predictability

The Principle: If your job is predictable, it's automatable.

What This Means:
- Seek roles involving novelty, exceptions, and unique situations
- Develop expertise in complex, context-dependent decisions
- Build careers around problems that don't have standard solutions

Examples:
- ❌ Data entry → ✓ Data interpretation and strategic recommendations
- ❌ Standard report generation → ✓ Crafting narrative insights for executives
- ❌ Following established procedures → ✓ Designing new processes for new situations

Design: Use checkmark/X symbols to show contrasts visually

- Develop reputation and personal brand
- Create value through connections and collaboration

Career Application:
- Attend industry events and conferences
- Maintain relationships with professors, mentors, colleagues
- Build presence in professional communities (LinkedIn, industry associations)
