## Detailed Schedule

### Individual Assignment - Reflective Essay (10%)

**Type:** Individual written assignment
**Length:** Maximum 1,500 words
**Topic:** Reflection on field trip (MISA Company visit) and guest speaker session (Ms. Ha Linh)
**Focus Question:** "What do these activities inform you about your preparation for your future career?"

**Due Date:** TBD (To be announced after field trip and guest speaker sessions complete)

**Submission Requirements:**
- Use reflective framework (Gibbs, Kolb, or Driscoll & Bolton)
- Submit via Moodle
- Format: .docx or PDF
- File naming: StudentNumber_FirstName_ReflectiveEssay_Date.docx

**Key Components:**
- Brief activity description (200-300 words)
- Critical reflection & analysis (900-1000 words)
- Future action planning (300-400 words)

### Mentorship Program (20%)

**Type:** Continuous assessment with multiple deliverables
**Duration:** 10 weeks (September 23 - November 30, 2025)
**Format:** Minimum 4 one-on-one meetings with industry mentor (at least 50% in-person)

**Program Coordinators:**
- Le Huong Lan (K66A)
- William Winterton Lang (K66B)

#### Deliverables & Timeline:

| Deliverable | Frequency | Submission Deadline | Submit To |
|-------------|-----------|---------------------|-----------|
| Pre-Meeting Preparation Form | Before each meeting | 48 hours before meeting | Mentor (via email) |
| Reflection Template | After each meeting | 48 hours after meeting | Program coordinator |
| Personal Development Plan (draft) | Once | Friday, November 28, 2025 | Program coordinator |

**Minimum Requirements:**
- Attend at least 4 mentoring meetings (80% attendance)
- Submit all 4 Pre-Meeting Preparation Forms (48 hours before)
- Submit all 4 Reflection Templates (48 hours after)
- Complete Personal Development Plan by program end
- Demonstrate progression across reflections

**Grading Components:**
- Reflection Portfolio (4 reflections showing progression): 40% of mentorship grade (8 marks)
- Personal Development Plan: 30% of mentorship grade (6 marks)
- Engagement & Documentation: 30% of mentorship grade (6 marks)

**Note:** Mentorship program = 20 marks total = 20% of overall PPD course grade

### Personal and Professional Development Plan (40%)

**Type:** Comprehensive career planning document
**Development:** Progressive throughout semester, finalized in Weeks 11-12

**Due Date:** Friday, November 28, 2025

**Required Components:**
1. Self-Assessment Summary (MBTI, VIA, EI, Learning Style, Holland Code)
2. SWOT Analysis (personal strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats)
3. Skills Gap Analysis
4. Vision Statement (50 words - 5-year professional vision)
5. SMART Goals:
   - Short-term (1 year): Minimum 3 goals
   - Medium-to-long-term (3-5 years): Minimum 3 goals
6. Mentor Integration (documented advice from 4 mentorship meetings)
7. Personal Reflection (200 words on authentic self and growth journey)
8. Commitment Statement

**Evaluation Criteria:**
- Depth of self-assessment integration
- SMART goal quality (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound)
- Mentor integration and evidence
- Realism balanced with ambition
- Emotional intelligence and soft skills focus
- Implementation planning (practical, actionable)

**Template:** Provided in course materials

---

### Final Oral Exam (30%)

**Type:** Individual presentation with Q&A
**Format:** Professional presentation (TED-talk style)
**Duration:** 15 minutes total
- 10 minutes: Presentation of Personal and Professional Development Plan
- 5 minutes: Q&A with instructors

**Date:** Exam period (specific time slots to be scheduled)

**Content Requirements:**
- Present your Personal and Professional Development Plan
- Articulate your values, strengths, and career vision
- Explain your 1-3-5 year goals and strategies
- Demonstrate self-awareness and professional communication
- Connect learning from course and mentorship to future plans

**Presentation Guidelines:**
- Professional slides (10-12 slides recommended)
- Clear structure (opening, body, conclusion)
- Evidence of preparation and polish
- Authentic delivery (not reading from script)
- Professional appearance and demeanor

**Evaluation Criteria:**
- Content depth and self-awareness
- Goal clarity and strategic thinking
- Presentation skills and delivery
- Response to questions
- Professional presence

**Rubric:** See detailed rubric in syllabus
