# Group Presentation

**Course:** BCI2AU - Business Communication
**Due:** Week 12
**Weight:** 25%

---

## Task Overview

Deliver a 15-minute team presentation followed by 10-minute Q&A analyzing a business communication challenge and presenting evidence-based recommendations. This capstone presentation synthesizes all course frameworks.

---

## Presentation Topics (Choose ONE)

**Topic A: Internal Communication Audit**

Analyze internal communication practices at a real organization (with permission) or case study organization. Present findings and recommendations to simulate a consulting engagement.

- Identify 3+ communication breakdowns or opportunities
- Apply course frameworks to diagnose root causes
- Present actionable recommendations with implementation plan
- Include visual examples (anonymized if real organization)

**Topic B: Cross-Cultural Communication Strategy**

A company is expanding from Vietnam to a new market (Japan, Germany, or USA). Present a communication strategy for the leadership team.

- Apply Hofstede's cultural dimensions
- Address specific communication adaptations needed
- Include stakeholder communication plan
- Provide training recommendations for staff

**Topic C: Crisis Communication Plan**

Develop a crisis communication plan for a specific scenario (product recall, data breach, or executive scandal). Present to the executive team.

- Apply crisis communication framework
- Include stakeholder messaging matrix
- Present timeline for 24-hour, 72-hour, 1-week responses
- Include media response guidelines

---

## Requirements

**Content Requirements:**

- [ ] Clear analysis using at least 3 course frameworks
- [ ] Evidence-based arguments (industry data, case studies)
- [ ] Specific, actionable recommendations
- [ ] Implementation considerations (timeline, resources, metrics)
- [ ] Balanced team participation (all members present)

**Presentation Requirements:**

- [ ] 15 minutes (±1 minute; penalties for over/under)
- [ ] Professional slide deck (10-15 slides)
- [ ] Strong opening hook and memorable closing
- [ ] Smooth transitions between speakers
- [ ] Confident delivery (voice, pace, eye contact)

**Q&A Requirements:**

- [ ] 10 minutes audience Q&A
- [ ] Demonstrate deep knowledge of topic
- [ ] Handle challenging questions professionally
- [ ] Team supports each other (not solo responses)

**Team Requirements:**

- [ ] 3-4 members per team
- [ ] Equal speaking time (±1 minute per member)
- [ ] Evidence of collaboration (not siloed sections)
