# Executive Summary

**Course:** BCI2AU - Business Communication
**Due:** End of Week 10
**Weight:** 10%

---

## Task Overview

Write a concise executive summary (250-350 words) synthesizing a complex business topic into actionable recommendations for a senior executive audience, followed by a framework reflection (150-200 words) demonstrating how you applied executive communication frameworks to achieve maximum impact. This capstone written assignment demonstrates your ability to communicate efficiently at the highest levels of business.

---

## Scenario (Choose ONE)

**Option A: Market Entry Recommendation**

A Vietnamese consumer goods company is considering entering the Thai market. You have been provided a 15-page market research report (attached on Moodle). Synthesize the key findings and provide a clear recommendation to the CEO.

- The CEO has 5 minutes to review before a board meeting
- Focus on: market opportunity, risks, resource requirements, recommendation
- Apply Pyramid Principle for instant comprehension
- Include only decision-critical information

**Option B: Crisis Response Summary**

A manufacturing company discovered a product quality issue affecting 5% of units. You have been provided an incident report and stakeholder communications log (attached on Moodle). Summarize the situation and recommend next steps for the COO.

- Immediate decisions needed within 24 hours
- Stakeholders include: customers, regulators, media, employees
- Prioritize actions by urgency and impact
- Apply crisis communication framework

**Option C: Strategic Initiative Review**

A technology startup's 6-month pilot of a new AI customer service system has concluded. You have been provided the pilot evaluation report (attached on Moodle). Summarize findings and recommend whether to scale, pivot, or discontinue.

- Board of Directors meeting in 1 week
- Key metrics: cost savings, customer satisfaction, employee adoption
- Present balanced analysis of pros and cons
- Clear recommendation with confidence level

---

## Requirements

**Content Requirements:**

- [ ] Clear recommendation in first sentence
- [ ] 3-4 key supporting points with evidence
- [ ] Quantified impact where possible
- [ ] Risk acknowledgment (1-2 sentences)
- [ ] Specific next steps/call to action

**Format Requirements:**

- [ ] 250-350 words (executive summary body)
- [ ] 150-200 words (framework reflection, separate section)
- [ ] Single page when printed (both sections combined)
- [ ] Bullet points for supporting details
- [ ] Bold or highlighted recommendation
- [ ] Professional executive formatting

**Reflection Requirements:**

- [ ] Explain how you applied Pyramid Principle to lead with recommendation
- [ ] Discuss your SCQA strategy for the specific scenario
- [ ] Reflect on concision decisions (what you excluded and why)
- [ ] Analyze how you adapted message for Executive Presence and C-suite audience
- [ ] Demonstrate understanding of information density and strategic synthesis

**Quality Standards:**

- [ ] Every word earns its place (no filler)
- [ ] Jargon-free or jargon-defined
- [ ] Self-sufficient (no need to read source document)
- [ ] Actionable (reader knows what to do next)
