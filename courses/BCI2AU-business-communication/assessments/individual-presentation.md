# Individual Presentation Grade

**Course:** BCI2AU - Business Communication
**Due:** Week 12
**Weight:** 15%

---

## Task Overview

Your individual presentation grade assesses your personal contribution and performance within the group presentation. This includes preparation, delivery quality, and team citizenship.

---

## Assessment Components

**Individual Delivery (10%):**

- Personal speaking quality (voice, pace, clarity)
- Eye contact and audience engagement
- Confidence and executive presence
- Handling of individual Q&A questions

**Team Contribution (5%):**

- Peer evaluation from team members
- Evidence of preparation and collaboration
- Support for other team members during presentation
- Constructive participation in Q&A

---

## Peer Evaluation

Each team member completes a confidential peer evaluation form assessing:

- **Preparation:** Did this person prepare thoroughly?
- **Collaboration:** Did this person contribute to team discussions?
- **Reliability:** Did this person meet deadlines and commitments?
- **Support:** Did this person help others succeed?

Peer evaluation scores are averaged and can adjust individual grade ±5%.

---

## Requirements

**Individual Performance:**

- [ ] Strong personal speaking quality
- [ ] Confident delivery with executive presence
- [ ] Effective eye contact and audience engagement
- [ ] Professional handling of Q&A questions
- [ ] Equal speaking time with other team members

**Team Citizenship:**

- [ ] Thorough preparation before presentation day
- [ ] Active collaboration during team meetings
- [ ] Meeting all team deadlines and commitments
- [ ] Supporting other team members during delivery
- [ ] Constructive participation in team Q&A
