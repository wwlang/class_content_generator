## Written Portfolio Details (35%)

### Business Memo: Audience Analysis (10%)
- **Due:** End of Week 4
- **Length:** 500-750 words
- **Format:** Professional memo format
- **Task:** Analyze a business communication challenge and propose an audience-centered approach
- **Assessment Focus:** Audience analysis frameworks, message adaptation, strategic thinking

### Persuasive Proposal (15%)
- **Due:** End of Week 7
- **Length:** 1000-1500 words
- **Format:** Formal business proposal
- **Task:** Develop a persuasive proposal for a business initiative using evidence-based frameworks
- **Assessment Focus:** Pyramid Principle, persuasion techniques, evidence integration

### Executive Summary (10%)
- **Due:** End of Week 10
- **Length:** 300-500 words
- **Format:** Executive summary
- **Task:** Synthesize a complex business topic into a clear, actionable executive summary
- **Assessment Focus:** Concision, clarity, executive communication style

## Presentation Details (40%)

### Group Presentation (25%)
- **Due:** Week 12
- **Duration:** 15 minutes + 10-minute Q&A
- **Team Size:** 3-4 members
- **Topic:** Business communication challenge analysis and recommendations
- **Assessment Focus:** Structure, delivery, visual aids, Q&A handling, team coordination

### Individual Presentation Grade (15%)
- **Assessed:** Within group presentation
- **Focus:** Individual preparation, personal delivery, team contribution
- **Includes:** Peer evaluation component

## Quiz Details (15%)

### In-Class Quiz
- **When:** Week 11 tutorial session
- **Duration:** 30 minutes
- **Format:** 25-30 multiple choice questions
- **Coverage:** All frameworks and concepts from Weeks 1-10
- **Materials:** Closed book

## Participation Details (10%)

### Attendance (5%)
- Taken within first 10 minutes of each class
- 2 absences allowed without penalty
- Each additional absence reduces grade

### Active Participation (5%)
- Complete all in-class activities
- Engage in discussions and workshops
- Professional conduct throughout
