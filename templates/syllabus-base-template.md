# **COURSE POLICIES**

## **Attendance Policy**

Attendance is mandatory and directly impacts your success in this course.

- Attendance will be taken within the first 10 minutes of each class
- Students arriving after attendance is taken will be marked absent
- Students who miss three consecutive classes without official documentation will be removed from the course
- Valid absences require documentation (medical certificate, official university business, etc.)

**Important:** Many in-class activities, workshops, and presentations cannot be made up. Plan to attend every session.

## **Academic Integrity**

All students must maintain the highest standards of academic honesty. Violations include:

- **Plagiarism:** Using others' words or ideas without proper citation
- **Cheating**: Unauthorized collaboration or use of materials during assessments
- **Fabrication**: Making up data, sources, or information

**Consequences**: Violations will result in a zero on the assignment and possible failure of the course, plus referral to university disciplinary procedures.

## **Group Work Policy**

For the group presentation:

- Groups must be formed with a maximum of 4 students by Week 4
- Non-contributing members may receive significantly reduced grades

## **Quiz Format**

Each quiz consists of:

- **25-30 multiple choice questions** testing:
  - Key concepts and definitions
  - Application of frameworks to scenarios
  - Analysis of communication examples
  - Best practices and principles
- **Duration:** 30 minutes
- **Coverage**: Lectures, assigned readings, in-class discussions

**Sample Question Types:**
***Recall**:* According to [framework], which element refers to [concept]? a) Option A b) Option B c) Option C d) Option D
***Application**:* You need to [scenario]. According to [technique], what should you do? a) Option A b) Option B c) Option C d) Option D
***Analysis**:* A company [action] during a [situation]. What [principle] is this violating? a) Option A b) Option B c) Option C d) Option D
