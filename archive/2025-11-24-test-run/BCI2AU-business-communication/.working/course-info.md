## Course Description (APPROVED)

This course develops strategic communication competencies essential for professional success in the modern business environment. Through a blend of theory and practice, students will master written, oral, digital, and interpersonal communication across diverse cultural and organizational contexts.

The course emphasizes hands-on application: students will create a portfolio of professional documents, deliver presentations, and engage with real business communication challenges. Special attention is given to emerging technologies (including AI), data storytelling, virtual collaboration, crisis management, and ethical communication in a globalized business world.
