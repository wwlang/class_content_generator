# Learning Objectives - BBAE-PPD

**Status:** Draft for Review
**Created:** 2025-11-05

## General Course Goal

This course is designed to help you develop the self-awareness, skills, and strategic mindset necessary to navigate your career successfully in a rapidly changing professional landscape.

## Learning Objectives by Category

### Understand & Apply

Upon successful completion of this course, students will be able to:

1. **Apply self-assessment tools** (MBTI, VIA Character Strengths, CliftonStrengths, Holland Code) to identify personal values, strengths, motivations, and career preferences
2. **Understand the impact of AI and technology** on workplace dynamics and future career opportunities, and identify key skills needed for working alongside AI
3. **Apply reflective learning models** (Gibbs' Reflective Cycle, Kolb's Experiential Learning Cycle) to extract meaningful insights from experiences
4. **Implement self-managed learning strategies** aligned with academic and career goals, including effective monitoring and adjustment techniques
5. **Apply time and energy management frameworks** (Eisenhower Matrix, time-blocking, habit stacking) to improve personal productivity

### Analyze & Evaluate

Upon successful completion of this course, students will be able to:

1. **Evaluate personal strengths and development areas** using multiple assessment tools and feedback from mentors and peers
2. **Analyze the transferable skills** required across different career contexts and assess personal competency gaps
3. **Critically assess personal assumptions and mindsets** (fixed vs. growth mindset) and their impact on learning and career development
4. **Evaluate workplace cultures and skill requirements** through field trip observations and guest speaker insights
5. **Analyze the relationship between emotional intelligence competencies** and professional effectiveness in diverse workplace situations

### Create & Present

Upon successful completion of this course, students will be able to:

1. **Design a comprehensive Personal and Professional Development Plan** with SMART goals and realistic 1-year, 3-year, and 5-year career roadmaps
2. **Create evidence-based reflections** that demonstrate progression in self-awareness, critical thinking, and integration of course concepts
3. **Develop and deliver a professional TED-talk format presentation** articulating personal values, career goals, and development strategies
4. **Construct a learning portfolio** documenting personal growth, mentor insights, and career development progress throughout the semester

### Collaborate

Upon successful completion of this course, students will be able to:

1. **Engage effectively in a mentorship relationship** by preparing meaningful questions, implementing mentor advice, and demonstrating professional communication
2. **Provide constructive peer feedback** using appropriate frameworks and professional language during group activities and presentations
3. **Build professional relationships** with industry mentors, guest speakers, and peers to expand career networks and learning opportunities
4. **Demonstrate cultural awareness and adaptability** in collaborative learning environments and professional interactions

## Alignment with Course Content

### Week-by-Week Mapping:

**Weeks 1-2:** Objectives related to AI/future of work, career skills
**Week 3:** Reflective learning models and writing
**Week 4:** Self-managed learning strategies
**Week 5:** Self-assessment tools, values, motivation
**Week 6:** Field trip - workplace observation and analysis
**Week 7:** Emotional intelligence and transferable skills
**Week 8:** Time and energy management
**Week 9:** Growth mindset and adaptability
**Week 10:** Entrepreneurship spirit and guest speaker
**Weeks 11-12:** Personal development plan creation and integration
**Throughout:** Mentorship engagement and reflection

## Alignment with Assessments

### Individual Assignment (10%):
- Reflective writing (Understand & Apply #3, Create & Present #2)
- Critical analysis (Analyze & Evaluate #4)
- Integration of course concepts (Analyze & Evaluate #5)

### Mentorship Program (20%):
- Mentorship engagement (Collaborate #1, #3)
- Reflection progression (Create & Present #2)
- Professional development (Analyze & Evaluate #1)

### Personal and Professional Development Plan (40%):
- SMART goals and roadmaps (Create & Present #1)
- Self-assessment integration (Understand & Apply #1, Analyze & Evaluate #1)
- Career strategy (Understand & Apply #2, Analyze & Evaluate #2)

### Final Oral Exam (30%):
- TED-talk presentation (Create & Present #3)
- Communication skills (Collaborate #2, #4)
- Synthesis and articulation (all categories)

## Research Basis

These objectives draw from:
- HBS: Authentic leadership, clarity of purpose and values
- Stanford GSB: Self-awareness as first leadership competency
- Wharton: Growth mindset, continuous learning
- MIT Sloan: Self-knowledge, intentional development
- Course materials: Weekly topics, assessments, mentorship structure
