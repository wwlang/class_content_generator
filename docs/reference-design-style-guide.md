### Primary Font: **Plus Jakarta Sans**
- **Usage:** Body text, descriptions, labels (54 uses)
- **Sizes:**
  - 18pt: Main body text, instructor info
  - 14pt: Content descriptions, bullet points
  - 11.9pt: Secondary descriptions
  - 8pt: Footer text, page numbers

### Display Font: **Cal Sans**
- **Usage:** Titles, headlines, large numbers (13 uses)
- **Sizes:**
  - 54.53pt: Large statistics
  - 48pt: Main slide titles
  - 44pt: Section headers
  - 43pt: Stats/numbers
  - 39.2pt: Title slide headlines

## üéØ Design Elements

### Spacing & Padding
- **Slide margins:** 0.49" from edges (left/right/top)
- **Footer positioning:** 7.00" from top (0.5" from bottom)
- **Card padding:** ~0.35-0.4" internal padding
- **Grid gaps:** 0.3-0.5" between columns

### Visual Elements
- **Icons:** Simple geometric shapes (circles, arrows) in orange
- **Cards:** White backgrounds with orange header bars
- **Decorative shapes:** Small abstract forms in corners
- **Images:** Large, full-bleed or contained in specific areas
- **Borders/Lines:** Minimal, mostly for card separation

### Text Hierarchy
1. **Slide Titles:** Cal Sans 36-48pt
2. **Section Headers/Stats:** Cal Sans 39-54pt, orange
3. **Subheadings:** Plus Jakarta Sans 14-16pt, bold
4. **Body Text:** Plus Jakarta Sans 13-14pt
5. **Secondary/Caption:** Plus Jakarta Sans 11-12pt
6. **Footer:** Plus Jakarta Sans 8pt
